## Possible Root Cause
NumPy nightly (`numpy 2.5.0.dev0`) is emitting a `DeprecationWarning` for use of the "generic" timedelta unit (message: `The 'generic' unit for NumPy timedelta is deprecated ... Please use a specific unit instead.`) when creating/using `np.datetime64("NaT")` or implicit unit `np.timedelta64` operations. Those warnings are being treated as errors by the pytest run (shown as `E   DeprecationWarning`), causing collection to fail and many tests to error out.
