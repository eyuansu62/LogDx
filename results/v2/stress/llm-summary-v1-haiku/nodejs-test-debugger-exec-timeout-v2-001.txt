## Possible Root Cause

The debugger test times out waiting for a response pattern that the debugger process never produces within 15 seconds. This suggests either a race condition in debugger startup, a hang in the debugger communication protocol, or a missing/delayed output from the debugger. The OpenSSL deprecation warnings are unrelated to this timeout; they reflect deprecated APIs in ncrypto that will require refactoring in future OpenSSL versions but do not cause the test failure.
